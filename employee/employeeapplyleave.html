Leave Policies
Casual leave (CL)
  • Eligibility:Casual leave is available to all regular employees of Waveworks who have completed their probationary period, which is usually 6 months from the date of hire.
  • Duration:Employees are entitled to 12 days of casual leave per calendar year.
Earned leave / Privilege leave (EL/PL)
  • Eligibility: All full-time and part-time employees are eligible for Earned leave / Privilege leave (EL/PL).
  • Accrual: Employees accrue vacation days based on their length of service, as follows:
  • Less than 1 year: 10 days per year
  • 1-5 years: 15 days per year
Sick Leave (SL)
  • Eligibility: All employees are eligible for sick leave.
  • Accrual: Sick leave is accrued at the rate of 1 day per month.
  • Request Procedure: Employees should notify their supervisor as soon as possible when taking sick leave.Medical documentation may be required for leaves exceeding 12 consecutive days.
Maternity/Paternity Leave (ML/PL)
  • Eligibility: Employees who have completed at least 12 months of service are eligible.
  • Duration: 24 weeks for maternity leave and 2 weeks for paternity leave.
  • Request Procedure: Employees should inform HR at least 2 weeks before the intended start date of leave and provide the necessary documentation.
Unpaid Leave (UL)
  • Eligibility:Unpaid leave is available to all regular full-time employees who have completed at least 12 months of continuous service with the company.
  • Duration: Unpaid leave can range from a minimum of 12 days to a maximum of 8 months.
Sabbatical Leave (SL)
  • Eligibility: Sabbatical leave is available to employees who have completed a specified number of years of service, typically ranging from 5 to 10 years.
  • Duration: 2 weeks for Sabbactical leave.
